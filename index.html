    The Rock Choir
    Website Description & More Info

    Rock Choir is a fun, non-auditioned choir that has grown since 2005 to over 16,000 members across the UK! We learn pop, gospel and motown songs by ear at our weekly rehearsals (so no requirement to read music!) A great place to try out new skills and to befriend local like-minded people. If you love to sing in the shower or dance at parties, this is the choir for you! We have also enjoyed local performances this year, such as singing at the Romsey Late Night Shopping event at Christmas, and surprising shoppers at WestQuay by doing a Flash Mob in aid of Sport Relief. Rock Choir also do big national events and have performed at the Hammersmith Apollo and at Wembley (more than once!) If you are interested why not come along to a FREE taster session? You can register and find out more information at www.rockchoir.com. Romsey Rock Choir rehearse on Tuesday evenings, 8-9.30pm at the Abbey United Reformed Church Hall in term-time.
