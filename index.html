Beggars Fair Youth Festival

July 13th 2013

About

The Beggars Fair is an annual music event but for the 5th time running the RYC (Romsey Youth Council) has stolen the park to lay on an event primarily aimed at young people!

This is an Alcohol free event. Anyone with alcohol or under the influence will be refused entry. The event is being monitored by police and security guards.

It's FREE to attend, is open air and found in Memorial Park park and has loads of young local music acts performing.

Bands/Artists Apply

After many requests and a lot of waiting we are able to start accepting bands!

To be elegeable to play we do have some rough rules we go by:

  • Band/artist be under the age of 21
  • No Explicit Content
  • Can be at the event for the whole day
  • Can play for upto 30 minutes if required

These rules can be waived at Romsey Youth Council's discretion.
